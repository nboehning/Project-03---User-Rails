To use the asset package you will need:
The player prefab in the scene(need to loose prefab connections after placing in hierarchy) 
Place canvas into scene from prefabs as well.
To open the editor click on player and click the open editor button.

Drag your waypoint objects into the editor to use them for your rails.

After building game place your exported levels into the folder with the exe.
Levels should be able to be selected inside game.
